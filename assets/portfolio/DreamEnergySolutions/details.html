Dream Energy Solutions
About

Dream Energy Solutions is a one-stop shop for all commercial energy needs; we broker and consult on all energy-related touchpoints, customized to unique business requirements. They are an independent energy brokerage, experts in negotiating and procuring the lowest natural gas & electricity rates for businesses across Ontario, Alberta, British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Quebec.

Implementation

Designed a brand new digital catalog, implemented a site-wide call to action, and implemented a brand new solution in line with the current site design. Custom module build-outs and integrations were implemented to accommodate the internal business flows. A custom library was integrated to allow the easier creation of many functions that were built for improved management. Built a system that provides greater transparency and far more efficient workflows while maintaining complete control over the internal business flows.
